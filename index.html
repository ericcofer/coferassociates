Who We Are

A Family Business
Built on Trust

Thank you for your interest in our company. In operation since 1971, Cofer Associates is a professional, full-service General Contractor and Handyman for both residential and commercial clients. We are a family-owned and operated business proudly serving Middlesex County, Metro West, the North Shore, and surrounding areas.

We have completed hundreds of projects throughout the region. Each one receives our complete attention to detail — from start to finish, your home or business will be held to the highest standards of construction and craftsmanship. Our clients expect nothing less.
