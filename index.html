Who We Are

A Family Business
Built on Trust

Thank you for your interest in our company. In operation since 1971, Cofer Associates is a professional, full-service General Contractor and Handyman for both residential and commercial clients. We are a family-owned and operated business proudly serving Middlesex County, Metro West, the North Shore, and surrounding areas.

We have completed hundreds of projects throughout the region. Each one receives our complete attention to detail — from start to finish, your home or business will be held to the highest standards of construction and craftsmanship. Our clients expect nothing less.

What We Do

Our Services

Additions & Remodeling

Whether you're expanding your footprint or transforming an existing space, we deliver seamless additions and renovations built to the highest standards — enhancing both function and lasting value.

Kitchens & Bathrooms

From custom cabinetry and countertops to tile, fixtures, and full gut renovations, we transform your kitchen and bathroom into spaces you'll love for years to come.

Basements & In-Law Dwellings

Unlock the hidden potential of your home with a finished basement or a fully appointed in-law suite — thoughtfully designed for comfort, privacy, and lasting quality.

Decks

Extend your living space outdoors with a custom-built deck crafted for beauty and durability. Built to withstand New England weather, your deck will be an asset and an enjoyment for decades to come.
